# <span data-lang="en">🎯 Projects</span><span data-lang="zh" hidden>🎯 项目</span>

1. <span data-lang="en"><strong><span style="font-size:1.15rem">Research on Variable Tension and Follow-Up Control of Suspension Systems Under Micro-Low Gravity Simulation Scenarios</span></strong><br>
<strong>Source:</strong> National Natural Science Foundation of China <strong><span style="color:#224B8D">(NSFC)</span></strong> Youth Student Basic Research Project <strong><span style="color:#224B8D">(for Ph.D. Students)</span></strong>; Jan. 2025–Dec. 2026; CNY 300,000; <strong><span style="color:#224B8D">Principal Investigator</span></strong>.<br>
<strong>Introduction:</strong> This project aims to achieve <strong><em>high-fidelity simulation</em></strong> of the equivalent mechanical environment of micro-low gravity fields using suspension systems, thereby providing feasible experimental conditions for ground-based studies of spacecraft dynamics on extraterrestrial bodies. Targeted methods for <strong>system design, modeling, and control</strong> are developed. The project outcomes have been practically implemented at the 518 Institute of the China Academy of Space Technology (CAST), which provides technical support for the ground-based validation of China’s extraterrestrial exploration missions.</span>
<span data-lang="zh" hidden><strong><span style="font-size:1.15rem">微低重力模拟场景下悬吊系统变拉力及随动控制研究</span></strong><br>
<strong>来源：</strong><strong><span style="color:#224B8D">国家自然科学基金</span></strong>青年学生基础研究项目<strong><span style="color:#224B8D">（博士研究生）</span></strong>；2025年1月—2026年12月；人民币30万元；<strong><span style="color:#224B8D">项目负责人</span></strong>。<br>
<strong>简介：</strong>本项目旨在利用悬吊系统实现微低重力场等效力学环境的<em>高保真模拟</em>，为在地面环境下研究航天器在地外天体表面的动力学行为提供可行的实验条件。项目有针对性地开发配套的<strong>系统设计、建模与控制</strong>方法。研究成果已在中国航天518研究所实际应用，为我国地外天体探测任务的地面验证工作提供技术支持。</span>


2. <span data-lang="en"><strong><span style="font-size:1.15rem">Dynamics-Mechanism-Driven General Planning and Control Methods for Underactuated Systems</span></strong><br>
<strong>Source:</strong> Ph.D. Dissertation Research; Sep. 2021–Jun. 2026.<br>
<strong>Introduction:</strong> This research aims to address the <strong>common challenges faced by various underactuated systems</strong>, in which the number of independent control inputs is <em>fewer</em> than the number of system degrees of freedom. By analyzing and exploiting their dynamic structural characteristics or common task requirements, this research develops <strong>a class of unified planning and control methods</strong> to enhance the steady/transient performance, safety, reliability, robustness, and intelligence of underactuated systems.</span>
<span data-lang="zh" hidden><strong><span style="font-size:1.15rem">动力学机理驱动的欠驱动系统通用规划控制方法研究</span></strong><br>
<strong>来源：</strong>博士学位论文研究课题；2021年9月—2026年6月。<br>
<strong>简介：</strong>本研究旨在解决不同欠驱动系统（独立控制输入数量少于系统自由度，即以“少”控“多”）中广泛存在的<strong>共性问题</strong>。通过分析并利用其动力学结构特征或共同任务需求，本研究设计<strong>一类通用的规划与控制方法</strong>，以提升欠驱动系统的稳/暂态性能、安全性、可靠性、鲁棒性及智能性。</span>
